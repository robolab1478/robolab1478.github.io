Land, Water, CO2, pollution

  • Land - Mines resources. Requires requires clearing and grading. Deforestation and loss of biodviersity
  • Water - Clearing and grading result in alteration of drainage channels which increases runoff and erosion. Chemical spills (for example, dust suppressants, dielectric) can contaminate water supplies. This can have an especially devastating effect on arid areas.
  • Carbon Dioxide - Mining resources and deforestation can increase carbon dioxide levels.

Net Energy

Net Energy is defined as "the ratio of energy required to release useable energy and the useful energy ."

Solar power has a net energy of 3:2
