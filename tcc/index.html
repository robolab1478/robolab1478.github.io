iPREVENT

iPREVENT is aimed at reducing the need of the EpiPen. Capitalism has taken over the brains of Mylan, so they're charging outstanding prices for these life-changing pens. The problem with EpiPens is there a one-and-done tool - once you use it once, you have to buy another one! The goal of iPREVENT is simple - reduce the number of times you need to use an EpiPen. Our idea is engineered to be simple - take a drop of our specially formulated non-toxic allergen indicitor, and place it a food item that could have the allergen. Based on the presence of the allergen, it will change colors, using the universal red is bad and green is good color scheme.
